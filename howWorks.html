How It Works: Features

Chore Planner

Give us how many people are in the house, what chores need to be done, how frequently they need to be done, and any special schedules that need to be accommodated, and we will do the rest! You'll be able to see what chores you need to do and if your roommates have completed their chores. As part of the sign-up process roommates will set expections for the chores and what is defined as "clean"


Group chat

We understand not all roommates are friends who are constantly in contact with each other. Along with the chore chart communication, there will be a built in chat feature.





Expense Tracker
